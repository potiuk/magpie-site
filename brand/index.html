Brand assets

The Apache Magpie™ logo, colors, typography, and ready-made social-media images. Everything here is released under the Apache License 2.0 for use when referring to the project — subject to the ASF trademark policy.

Logo & mark

The mark is the letter M holding a magpie. Use the mark on its own for square contexts (avatars, app icons) and the wordmark or full lockup where there is room. Keep clear space around the logo equal to the height of the bird's eye, and never recolor, rotate, stretch, or add effects to it.

Social media kit

Ready-to-upload avatars and banners at each platform's recommended size. The avatar is the mark on a white tile so it stays legible inside circular crops; the banner is the full lockup on the brand gradient. Keep key content centered — some platforms overlay the avatar on the lower-left of the banner.

Naming & usage

  • · Full name on first mention: Apache Magpie. “Magpie” alone is fine afterward.
  • · It's a project of the Apache Software Foundation — write “Apache Magpie,” not “Apache's Magpie.”
  • · Don't alter the mark's colors or proportions, or use it to imply endorsement of a third-party product.
